Главная Ask a psychologist

Ask a psychologist

  • Is there a question you always wanted to ask a psychologist?
  • Is there something that bothers you from time to time?
  • Is there anything that makes you sleep bad at night?
  • Arrived to such a point in life when you are stuck and need professional advice?
  • Or maybe there is just some weird question you want to be answered?

Feel free to ask anything you want or ever wanted to ask a psychologist, but... was ashamed to ask, or simply never had a chance to ask.

Some questions will be answered in this Q&A section. The most interesting questions I will be answering on my Youtube channel.

You will receive an email confirming that I have actually read your question and another email when an answer to your question is published.

Make sure you enter the correct and valid email address because otherwise your question will be ignored.

Remember

You have to describe your psychological issue or a challenge (most people call it a "problem", but I suggest to call such situations as a challenge) which you want to treat or solve. After that please clearly state a question or questions you want me to answer.

Note

As I have clearly outlined in my rules: Confidentiality is a must from both sides! Which means - your surname (should you wish to write it), email and location (except country) WILL NEVER BE PUBLISHED!
